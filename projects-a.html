KULLA - 2D to 3D Conversion Platform

Technologies: Django, NextJS, Flask & Blender (microservice), ArgoCD, Docker, PostgreSQL, Google OAUTH2

Overview: Kulla is a cloud-based platform designed to bridge the gap between 2D and 3D construction workflows, particularly in the architectural and construction sectors. The software allows users to continue working with traditional 2D tools like AutoCAD while automatically converting floor plans into 3D Building Information Modeling (BIM) formats. This enhances collaboration and communication between stakeholders, helping reduce errors and delays in construction projects.

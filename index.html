Our Story

Softwine's journey began with a simple observation that would revolutionize how businesses approach software architecture and DevOps.

Our founder, Bezalel Brandwine, had spent years as a hands-on software architect in a fast-paced cloud startup. During this time, he noticed a recurring pattern: many companies were struggling to maintain in-house software architects and DevOps teams, often for infrastructure and pipelines that, once properly set up, required only periodic maintenance.

Bezalel also recognized a pervasive misconception in the industry: many companies viewed new tools and technologies as complicated and time-consuming to implement. In reality, these modern solutions, when properly understood and applied, significantly simplify processes and accelerate development. These insights led Bezalel to envision a new approach. What if there was a way to provide businesses with expertly crafted software infrastructure and DevOps solutions that could be set up once and maintained efficiently? This would free companies to focus on their core business without the overhead of full-time, specialized tech teams.

In 2024, Bezalel founded Softwine with a mission to make high-quality software architecture and DevOps accessible to businesses of all sizes. Like a fine wine that's carefully crafted and aged to perfection, Softwine's solutions are meticulously designed, implemented, and maintained to provide lasting value.

Today, Softwine continues to grow, partnering with businesses across industries to create robust, scalable, and efficient software infrastructures. Our team of experts brings the sophistication of advanced software architecture with the approachability of a trusted partner, ensuring that our clients can harness the full potential of technology without the complexity.

At Softwine, we're not just building software solutions; we're cultivating digital ecosystems that help businesses thrive in an increasingly connected world.
